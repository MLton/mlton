This page is for experimental versions of MLton. These versions are not as well tested as our public releases, and may not be available for our all our usual platforms.

20030720 makes the following changes:

  • renames _ffi as _import
  • adds cdecl and stdcall attributes to _import and _export expressions
20030720 is available in the following formats:
  • Binary
    • tgz for Cygwin/Windows
    • tgz for Linux (glibc 2.2)
    • rpm for Red Hat 7.x, 8.x, 9.x
  • Source
    • tgz for any platform
    • srpm for Red Hat 7.x, 8.x, 9.x
